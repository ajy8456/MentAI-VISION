Overview

There is an inconvenience in having to visit specialized institutions (counseling centers, hospitals) to address the socially prominent issue of mental health. SK Telecom has developed the "My Mental Wellness Check-in" service, which allows users to comfortably check their mental health (depression, anxiety, stress) in daily life through mobile devices and PCs and receive personalized care program recommendations. We developed "MentAI VISION," a mental health analysis technology (depression, anxiety, stress) using Multimodal AI technology that utilizes voice and facial video, the core modalities of telecommunications services. This is integrated with the group company's employee wellness platform "Happy Wings" to provide tailored care programs. The service aims to lower the physical and psychological barriers to mental health management and shift the paradigm from treatment to prevention-focused care.

Usage Scenario

Service Demo Video

The My Mental Wellness Check-in service can be easily used in daily life through mobile devices or PCs without the need to visit hospitals or counseling centers directly. Compared to lengthy surveys that take over 10 minutes, users can check their mental state by recording their voice and facial video in just 2 minutes.

  • Users record four sentences by following guides to analyze depression and anxiety. To enhance user experience, the volume of the recording voice is visualized in the center of the screen during recording.
  • Users record one minute of the their facial video to measure heart rate variability (HRV), which is used to assess stress levels. To ensure accurate measurement, the system tracks the position of the eyes and nose and automatically pauses recording if these positions move out of the frame.
  • After measurements, the service provides a comprehensive score along with numerical indexes for depression, anxiety, and stress, presented alongside group average values for reference. Users can also track the trends of their measurements over time.
  • Based on the results, related programs provided by HappyWings are recommended. To obtain more accurate results, it is recommended to use the service in a quiet office or home environment.

Technical Details

The depression and anxiety analysis technology used in the My Mental Wellness Check-in service, called MentAI VISION, utilizes an AI model developed by SK Telecom (Spectral Image-Based Diagnosis of Voice Disorders: Leveraging Spectrograms for Non-Invasive Assessment, NeurIPS 2023 Workshop). The model, along with analyzing the userâ€™s voice waveform itself, and converts the voice into frequency images (spectrograms). It then encodes image patches from multiple frequency bands using computer vision techniques to classify target voice disorders. MentAI VISION is a variant of this model trained on mental health data, achieving a recall rate of approximately 89% in researcher clinical studies. Additionally, the technology includes language semantic analysis using speech-to-text and large language models tailored to specific tasks (e.g., self-introduction) for mental health assessment. SK Telecom has filed 11 related domestic patents and 4 international patents (PCT/KR2022/014579, PCT/KR2024/003366, PCT/KR2024/003745, PCT/KR2025/007963) for these technologies. The stress analysis technology is developed by the partner company Clify. It uses AI to predict blood flow from facial video footage and measures heart rate variability (HRV) to analyze stress levels.
